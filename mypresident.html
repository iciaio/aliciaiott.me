ALICIA IOTT

My President, 2016
Collaborators: Anna Schulz, Hizal Celik

This project consists of fake website with content about an interactive art project called "My President" in which twitter users control the speech of a Donald Trump avatar. I was inspired by social media platforms after the 2016 presidential election. Sites like twitter aim to give the common citizen a voice. After reading dozens of #NotMyPresident tweets, I wondered how a perfect democracy could reflect the often conflicting views of the populus. Here are the full Buzzfeed website mockup and Creators Project website.
