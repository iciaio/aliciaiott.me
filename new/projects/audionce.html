Audionce (2015)

An iOS application for pinning sounds to geolocations in the world.

Once a user records and places a sound at their current geoloction, other app users within a close range of the submit location can hear the sound until it is deleted or overwritten. (Newer sounds will overwrite old sounds if dropped in the same location.)

This app was intended to imitate the core aspects of graffiti, but with sounds instead of visual art. I oversaw the production of the android version of this app by Steven Rosendahl.
