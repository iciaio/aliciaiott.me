Dranimate, 2016

Dranimate is a interactive animation system which allows people to control animations with hand gestures. In an effort to make the app more accesible, (using webcam functionality instead of leap motion) I worked to implement fingertip tracking in javascript.Using background deletion and adaptive thresholding I was able to identify the contour of a hand, etc. I also wrote a color-tracking version in which users need to wear colored markers on their fingertips, which was more robust.
