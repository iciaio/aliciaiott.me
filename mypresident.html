My President, 2016

I came up with this concept a few days after the 2016 US Presidential election. I was inspired by social media platforms, like twitter, which aim to give the common citizen a voice. After reading dozens of #NotMyPresident tweets, I wondered what it would be like if democracy had been effective in providing a voice for the populus. I wanted to allow people to see a president that had the same thoughts and values as themselves. With the help of Anna Schulz and Hizal Celik, this project was implemented as a sort of speculative design art work. Here are the full Buzzfeed website mockup and Creators Project website.
