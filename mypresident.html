ALICIA IOTT

My President, 2016
Collaborators: Anna Schulz, Hizal Celik

This project consists of fake website with content about an interactive art project called "My President" in which twitter users control the speech of a Donald Trump avatar. I came up with this concept a few days after the 2016 US Presidential election. I was inspired by social media platforms, like twitter, which aim to give the common citizen a voice. After reading dozens of #NotMyPresident tweets, I wondered how a perfect democracy could reflect the often conflicting views of the populus. I wanted to allow people to see a president that had the same thoughts and values as themselves. Here are the full Buzzfeed website mockup and Creators Project website.
