ALICIA IOTT






The Girls Are Home, 2016
Collaborators: Char Stiles

This is an interactive dollhouse in which the windows are lenses into a 3d recorded scene being played back on mobile phones within the house. I modeled the house from memory of my childhood home. Steroscopic footage is displayed behind the windows. The footage is a mundane represenation of domestic life shot in our own home. The viewer is placed into the story and given a role (as a giant, voyeuristic intruder) in this non-linear immersive storytelling device.

This piece was exhibited at WEIRD REALITY (an Art and Code symposium) at Carnegie Mellon University.
