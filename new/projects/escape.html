Escape

A virtual reality video game made for oculus.

This game mimics the repetition of everyday life. A player will walk through the same house multiple times. Each door leads back into the same house, but on each pass the house will be slightly different. For example a drawer that is usually closed and locked may be open to reveal a clue or a key. The goal is to end the repetition by leaving the house. To do so the player must find keys, open locks, or read letters, etc.
