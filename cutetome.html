ALICIA IOTT

2016 Cute To Me
Collaborators: Char Stiles, Guy DeBree, Kevin Ramser, David Gordon

This was an experimental animated music video starring local pittsburgh drag artist, Moonbaby. I worked in a team of 5 to 3D-scan, motion-capture, and record in a panoptic dome the performances of Moonbaby. In post production we warped her virtual image to fantastically bucksome and impossibly slick, to a deformed 3d shape beyond reconition.
