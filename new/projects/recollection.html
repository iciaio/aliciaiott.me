Recollection (2015)

A series of drawings which amplify certain details of my childhold home.

This project was the very beginning of my exploration of memory. I used the texture of paint (only the texture as the paint is white) to replicate certain textures that are poignant in my memory of my childhood home. The tactile sensation is very nostalgic to me. Rather than hoping to acheive an accurate portrayal, I tried to evoke a child's perspective and memory with collage, skewed perspectives, inconsistant scale. I magnified what I remembered well and omitted what I did not.
